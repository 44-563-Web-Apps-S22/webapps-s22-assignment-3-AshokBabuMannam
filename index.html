Ashok Babu Mannam

Paraside is my favourite place to have food.

This restuarent is famous for Hyderabad biryani, which is near by Parde ground

Directions to the reach the Place

Rajiv Gandhi International Airport

  1. After coming out of Airport.
  2. Book a cab from OLA/Uber to destination as Paraside.
  3. Reserve a table via Online for dining.
  4. After reaching the destination.
  5. Show the reserved table details you booked.
  6. Have your seat and ask for Menu card.
  7. Order the list of items you wish to eat and enjoy the food.
  • Below are the food items, I recommend to try atleast once

      • Chicken Dum Biryani
      • Chicken SP Biryani
      • Mutton Biryani
      • Prawn Biryani
